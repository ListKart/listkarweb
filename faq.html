üîí Privacy & Data (Best in Class)

Q: Do you track what I buy or my shopping habits? A: Absolutely NOT. We do not track, analyze, or even see what you buy. Your shopping lists are yours alone. Unlike other apps, we don't build a profile of your habits to sell to advertisers.
Q: Do I need to create an account to use the app? A: No. You can use all features of ListKart without ever creating an account. This guarantees your complete anonymity. We don't ask for your email, phone number, or social media login, so we have no way to link your data to "you".
Q: Is there a "hidden profile" of me? A: None. Since there is no login and no account, there is technically no way for us to build a profile on you. You are completely invisible to us.
Q: What happens when I share a list? A: When you share a list, only that specific list is uploaded securely to allow your friends to see it. It is not linked to any personal identity or profile. Once the sharing purpose is done, it remains just a piece of data without an owner.
Q: Is my personal data safe? A: Yes. Your data primarily lives locally on your device. This is the safest place for it. We don't store your personal library on our servers, so it can't be leaked or stolen from us.
Q: Why does the app need camera access? A: We only use the camera to scan text from physical lists to help you digitize them quickly. We never record, save, or transmit any video or images from your camera. It is strictly for local processing.
Q: Do you sell my data to advertisers? A: No, never. We built ListKart to be a tool for you, not a data collection tool for us. We do not sell any data to third parties.
Q: Can I use the app offline? A: Yes! ListKart is designed to be offline-first. You can do everything without internet. Internet is only used if you explicitly choose to share a list or give feedback.
Q: How do I delete my data? A: Since your data is on your phone, you have full control. Uninstalling the app or clearing its data permanently wipes everything. We hold nothing back.
