How ListKart Helps You Stay Within Limit

1 Create a List & Set Your Limit

Start your shopping journey by setting a Budget Limit for your current list. Maybe it's 5000 for your monthly pantry restock, or 1000 for a quick vegetable run. By establishing a clear boundary right at the top, you anchor your spending mindset.

2 Enter Prices Automatically

As you add items (like Milk, Bread, or Detergent), you can quickly tap and add approximated or exact prices. Knowing that you've just added 300 worth of snacks suddenly makes you reconsider that extra box of chocolates.

3 Real-Time Spend vs. Limit View

While you shop, ListKart continuously calculates your total. Instantly see how much of your set limit is already consumed showing exactly how much margin you have left.

For Example:

Limit: 2000 | Current Cart: 1450

Safe Zone: You still have 550 left!

Visualizing Your Spending NEW GRAPHICS

Numbers are great, but visuals are better. ListKart features beautiful and interactive graphic reports to help you analyze your purchasing habits instantly.
